<!-- RATIONALE -->
<section class="section">
    <h2>Scientific Rationale</h2>
    <p>
    Epilepsy remains characterized by strong inter-individual variability in drug response.
    CANDLE addresses this limitation by integrating neuro-inspired computational models with 
    preclinical and clinical data, enabling mechanistic understanding and predictive optimization 
    of therapeutic strategies.
    </p>
</section>

<!-- OBJECTIVES -->
<section class="section">
    <h2>Objectives</h2>
    <div class="grid">

            <p>Multimodal integration of electrophysiological and clinical datasets.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="card">
            <h3>Modeling</h3>
            <p>Development of biophysical and reduced computational models of epileptic networks.</p>
        </div>

        <div class="card">
            <h3>Prediction</h3>
            <p>Optimization of pharmacological strategies using model-driven inference.</p>
        </div>
    </div>
</section>
